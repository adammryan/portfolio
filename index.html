Welcome!

Say hello to Adam! Adam is a freelance photographer (not really) who is working on his computer science degree at Brigham Young University. One class of his is about learning to build websites so he decided to take up photography instead. The world may never know if this is the right move for him.

Adam specializes in landscape photography for famous pop culture characters who want sentimental pictures in their homes. His most recent success was working with Jim Hopper in Hawkins Indiana, capturing many of the Stranger Things that were happening in town as stunning pictures.

Just to be clear, none of these pictures are actually taken or being promoted by Adam. This is in fact an assignment for class.
